Shooting Game AI


After playing like a thousand shooting games and still not being able to win like my friends, I started wondering of the ways I can change it. Then the idea of this AI came to my mind. Since I had already done a couple of AI projects, I wasn't too scared to start this one. And that's what gave birth to this project.

Shooter AI Version 1.0
An AI that automatically detects the enemy on the screen and shoots at their heads.

The 1st version will only move the mouse and make headshots. It would be pretty much like a Aiming AI.
The AI will be able to detect the enemy and shoot at their head.
I have already tried using the Python Pyautogui library and feeded it a lot of data but it didn'y give high accuracy. So, I am gonna have to train an ML model which is able to determine the enemy's location and shoot at it.

Steps:

  • Get lots of labelled data to train the ML model
  • Locate the head of the enemy
  • Hover to aim at head(keypad hovering is disabled in some games)
  • Shoot continuosly with smaal breaks to reduce recoil

Future Plans: Adding the feature of allowing the AI to move around in the game.
